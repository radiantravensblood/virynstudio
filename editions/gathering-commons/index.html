About Active Brain Community

A resource commons for people carrying care through real life.

Active Brain Community is imagined as a calm, practical, dignity-centered resource and connection hub for dementia caregivers, families, and community partners.

It would not replace medical care, emergency services, legal advice, or professional support. Its role is to reduce isolation, organize trustworthy starting points, and create well-held ways for people to learn from one another.

Community boundary

Peer experience can offer language, recognition, and practical ideas. It cannot evaluate an emergency or make clinical decisions.
