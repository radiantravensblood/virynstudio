# Studio Edition 01 — Gathering Commons

## Demonstration identity

**Active Brain Community** is a fictional concept for a calm, dignity-centered resource and connection hub serving dementia caregivers, families, and community partners.

## What this edition tests

- Self-orientation without collecting visitor data
- Filterable resource-library structure
- Private-by-design caregiver reflection
- Consent-based story intake and review language
- Facilitated, time-bounded support circles instead of an infinite feed
- Printable toolkit placement and future Gumroad delivery
- Three authored atmospheres: Hearth, Grove, and Dusk

## Content and safety boundary

The current copy is fictional demonstration content. The site does not provide medical, emergency, legal, or clinical advice. Forms run only in the browser and do not transmit or store responses.

A production build would require:

1. Caregiver and subject-matter interviews
2. Resource review and maintenance ownership
3. Moderation and escalation policy
4. Privacy, retention, deletion, and consent requirements
5. Local emergency and crisis-routing logic
6. Accessible event registration and calendar integration
7. Secure form processing before collecting personal information

## Suggested production spine

- **Content:** a small CMS or reviewed static resource library
- **Circles:** a styled registration interface over Google Calendar or a scheduling service
- **Operations:** spreadsheet/database as the source of truth; dashboard as a view
- **Stories:** private intake queue with explicit consent state and human editorial review
- **Downloads:** direct files for free resources; optional Gumroad fulfillment for expanded kits
