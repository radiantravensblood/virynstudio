Why Common Table exists

Practical care should feel clear, dignified, and possible to receive.

We help neighbors find food, transportation, household supplies, and small emergency grants while giving volunteers a clear path into useful work.

The framework keeps the organization’s mission visible without hiding the details people need: who a program is for, what happens next, and how privacy is protected.
